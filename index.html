Golf Calculator Privacy Policy

Effective: May 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Golf Calculator does not collect any personal information from users.

This app is a simple calculator for golf wind shots, power, and putting. No user accounts, registration, or data storage is required.

2. Permissions

The app requests no permissions and does not access device features like location, camera, contacts, or storage.

3. Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used in this app.

4. Data Storage

All calculations are performed locally on the device. No data is sent to servers or stored persistently.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page periodically for changes.

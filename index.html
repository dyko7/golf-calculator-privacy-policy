About Golf Calculator

Golf Calculator is a utility application designed to help golf game players calculate wind effects, shot power, elevation adjustments, putting values, and other gameplay-related calculations.

Key features include:

  • Golf guide search tools
  • Wind and putting aim calculators
  • Shot and putting power calculators
  • Uphill and downhill adjustment support
  • Customizable club and putter settings
  • Fast real-time calculations
  • Simple and user-friendly interface

The App is designed to operate entirely on your device and does not require user registration, login credentials, or an internet connection for core functionality.

Information We Collect

Golf Calculator does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personally identifiable information.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information
  • Device identifiers
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or photos

No user account is required to use the App.

Local Processing

All calculations and user-selected settings are processed locally on your device.

No gameplay calculations, club settings, putter settings, or other user-generated information are transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

Golf Calculator does not request access to:

  • Photos or media files

The App operates without requiring special device permissions.

Third-Party Services

Golf Calculator does not use:

The App does not display advertisements and does not use third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Security

Children's Privacy

Golf Calculator is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal information is collected by the App, users of all ages may use the application without providing personal data.
